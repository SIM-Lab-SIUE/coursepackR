# Undergraduate Group White Paper Writing Guide

This guide provides detailed instructions for writing your professional, data-driven white paper in Quarto.

## Structure
- Introduction: Establish problem and significance
- Literature Review: Synthesize sources, identify gap
- The Present Study: State RQs/RHs
- Methodology: Participants, Measures, Procedure, Analytic Strategy
- Results: Data analysis, tables, figures
- Discussion: Main findings, recommendations
- Conclusion: Key takeaway
- References: APA or journalistic formatted
- Appendix: Survey instrument

For section-by-section tips, see the template and technical guide.
