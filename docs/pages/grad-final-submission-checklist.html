Common Issues to Watch For

  • ❌ Missing citations or incomplete references
  • ❌ Inconsistent APA formatting
  • ❌ Broken links or missing files in GitHub
  • ❌ Missing data visualizations
  • ❌ Unformatted tables or figures
  • ❌ Rendering errors in Quarto
  • ❌ Word count too short or too long
