# Graduate Research Paper Writing Guide

This guide provides detailed instructions for writing your APA-style research paper in Quarto.

## Structure
- Introduction: Establish problem and significance
- Literature Review & Theoretical Framework: Synthesize sources, identify gap, choose theory
- The Present Study: State RQs/RHs
- Method: Participants, Measures, Procedure, Analytic Strategy
- Results: Data analysis, tables, figures
- Discussion: Main findings, limitations, future directions
- Conclusion: Key takeaway
- References: APA formatted
- Appendix: Survey instrument

For section-by-section tips, see the template and technical guide.
