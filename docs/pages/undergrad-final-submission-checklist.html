Common Issues to Watch For

  • ❌ Missing citations or unformatted references
  • ❌ Inconsistent formatting or heading levels
  • ❌ Missing data visualizations
  • ❌ Unreadable or poorly labeled figures/tables
  • ❌ GitHub repository not shared with all team members
  • ❌ Rendering errors in Quarto
  • ❌ Spelling and grammar errors
  • ❌ Missing sections or incomplete content
  • ❌ Unresolved merge conflicts in Git

Dividing Work Effectively

Suggested team breakdown:

  • Person A: Introduction & Literature Review
  • Person B: Methods & Results
  • Person C: Discussion, References & Proofing
  • All: Design visualizations, proof final copy

Questions or Problems?

If something doesn’t work: 1. Check the Troubleshooting Guide 2. Review the Undergraduate Technical Guide 3. Review the GitHub Guide for collaboration tips 4. Ask your instructor
