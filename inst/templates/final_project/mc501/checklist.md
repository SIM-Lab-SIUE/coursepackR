# Master Project Checklist

## Setup & Planning
- [ ] Finalize your research topic.
- [ ] Get your topic and RQs/RHs approved by your instructor.
- [ ] Create your private GitHub repository.
- [ ] Clone the repository to your local machine via RStudio.
- [ ] Set up your RStudio Project and main `research_paper.qmd` file.
- [ ] Set up your Zotero library and link your `references.bib` file.

## Research & Data Collection
- [ ] Conduct your literature review, adding 15-20 sources to Zotero.
- [ ] Write the draft of your Introduction & Literature Review.
- [ ] Design your survey.
- [ ] Ethics Check: Get your survey questions approved by your instructor.
- [ ] Launch your survey and collect data (Goal: 100+ responses).
- [ ] Close the survey and export the data as `survey_data_raw.csv`.
- [ ] Add the raw data file to your `/data` folder and push to GitHub.

## Analysis & Writing
- [ ] Write the `Method` section in APA style.
- [ ] Clean your data using an R script or code chunk.
- [ ] Analyze your data in R to answer your 2 RQs/RHs.
- [ ] Create at least 2 APA-formatted visualizations in R.
- [ ] Write the `Results` section, embedding your R output and visuals.
- [ ] Write the `Discussion` section, connecting your results to your lit review and theory.

## Finalization
- [ ] Write the `Abstract`, `Conclusion`, and format the `References` page.
- [ ] Read and edit the entire paper for clarity, style, and APA formatting.
- [ ] Check that all 15-20 sources are cited correctly.
- [ ] Check that your R code runs and your visualizations render properly.
- [ ] Render your final `research_paper.pdf` and add it to the `/output` folder.
- [ ] Update your `README.md` with your final abstract.
- [ ] Push all final changes to GitHub.
- [ ] Submit the `research_paper.pdf` to Blackboard.
- [ ] Submit your GitHub repository link.
